AI Ping-pong Game

Instructions

1. First keep your screen straight.
2. Make yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop
3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner a red dot should appear on the right wrist.
4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
